Our Origin

"What if Germany had won the First World War?" This question has been frequently raised since the end of World War 1. How much will a German victory in Europe change the life of people living under a rather different order? Perhaps there will be no conflict among those radical ideologies. Will there be a longer prosperity? Well, guess you can find a part of answer here. We proudly present to you--Kaisereich!

In Kaisereich we prepare themed trip for anyone who wants to know the answer above. Designated in an alternative timeline where Germany won World War 1, the trips will provide you with in-depth immersive experience in an imaginary germanized part of Eastern European! From Cuisine to sailing trips, everything you want, everything you may not even think of--All in Kaisereich!
